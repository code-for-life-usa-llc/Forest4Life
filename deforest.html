Summary on deforestation

Deforestation is the act of clearing large areas of forest. Deforestation has been going on for a really long time people have been cutting down large amounts of trees for millennia. But it started to accelerate in 8,000 bc and the 1900s those times were when half of the forest loss happened. People used deforestation for a lot of different things like

These things include

  1. Fuel
  2. Space for agriculture
  3. Manufacturing and construction
  4. Animal grazing
